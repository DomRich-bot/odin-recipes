Birria Tacos

Description

A delicious and easy beef taco recipe, slow roasted to bring out the incredible flavor. Make it once and you'll never go back to your boring old tacos. The unique sweet,spicy,and tangy flavor profile will leave you craving more. Pro tip, cook in your Instant pot to shave hours off of the cook time. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Beef Chuck Roast
  • Water
  • 6-8 Garlic Cloves
  • 1-2 Onions
  • 6-8 Ancho Chilis
  • Beef Bouillon
  • Sazon
  • Bay Leaves
  • Tomato Paste - 1 tbsp

Directions

  1. Cut Beef into large cubes,add them to pot and cover with water
  2. Add peeled garlic, de-seeded chilis, and onions
  3. Add tomato paste and beef bouillon(3-6 depending on size)
  4. Season with garlic powder,onion powder,salt,pepper, and Sazon
  5. Count and add bay leaves(must be removed after cooking)
  6. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 4 hours on stove top.(40-60 min in Instant Pot
  7. After cooked remove onions, garlic,a dn chilis and blend with a few tbsps of broth
  8. Remove Bay leaves and shred the beef
  9. Add blended mixture back to beef and broth
  10. Dip your tortillas in the broth and fry with the meat and mozzarella cheese
  11. Serve with fresh cilantro, enjoy your tacos!
