Oreo Milkshake Cookies

Description

This frosted oreo cookie recipe is simple and creative. The sweet vanilla frosting and oreo cookie base pair perfectly to bring the flavors of an oreo shake right to your plate!

Dough Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/2 cup Shortening
  • 1 tbsp Water
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla
  • 3 cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 1/2 cup Oreos

Frosting Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 2 cups Powdered Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2-4 tbsp Water

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Beat butter,shortening, water and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add vanilla and eggs, then mix until combined.
  4. Add baking powder,salt,flour,and oreos. Mix until combined.
  5. Scoop dough and flatten each dough ball, cook for 12 minutes.
  6. Put cookies directly in the fridge after baking to cool.
  7. For the frosting,whip together butter,powdered sugar, and vanilla for 2 minutes.
  8. Slowly add 2 tbsp of water and whip for 5 minutes until smooth.
  9. Frost the cooled cookies and enjoy!
