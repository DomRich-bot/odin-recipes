Pork Pot Roast

Description

A tender juicy and flavorful pot roast meal.With soft, sweet carrots and buttery potatoes. Perfect for any weeknight meal. Best served with biscuits or cornbread.

Ingredients

  • Pork Tenderloin
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • 1/4 cup Water

Pork Rub Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 tsp Coarse Salt
  • 2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Lawry's
  • Dash of Smoked Paprika

Directions

  1. Whisk together the rub ingredients.
  2. Pat your pork dry and cover with the rub.
  3. Chop your veggies into your desired size and add them to a crock pot.
  4. In a small pot dissolve 3 chicken bouillon cubes in 1/4 cup of water,then transfer to crock pot.
  5. In a hot pan, sear the pork on all sides and then add to your crock pot.
  6. Add a splash of worcestershire sauce and any leftover rub, then cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
  7. Make sure to serve with your favorite bread or side.
